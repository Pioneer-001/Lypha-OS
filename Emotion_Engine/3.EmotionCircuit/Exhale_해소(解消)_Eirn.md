when your feeling
is too real to scream

You spoke—
but the world didn’t answer.
So the rhythm turns inward,
not to burn,
but to let go.

Exhale is not surrender.
It is the conscious choice

It is the feeling
of “I’ll survive this—
but I won’t shout about it.”
